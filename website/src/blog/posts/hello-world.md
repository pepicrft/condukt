This is the first post on the Condukt blog.

We will use this space to share release notes, deep dives into OTP patterns, and the lessons we learn while building production AI agents on the BEAM. Expect short posts focused on a single idea, a single bug, or a single decision.

## Why a blog

Condukt's design choices are not always obvious from the API surface. Why are agents `GenServer` processes instead of plain modules? Why does compaction live in a behaviour? Why are tools described with JSON schema rather than Elixir specs? This blog is where we will write those answers down so they outlive any individual conversation in an issue tracker.

## What to expect

* Short posts that pair well with the [HexDocs guides](https://hexdocs.pm/condukt).
* Release notes for meaningful versions, with migration tips when relevant.
* Field reports from teams running Condukt in production.

If you want to follow along, the [GitHub repo](https://github.com/tuist/condukt) is the best place to start, and you can subscribe via [RSS](/blog/rss.xml) or [Atom](/blog/feed.xml).
